The SAIBAY Process

From first call to signed proposal in 14 days.

Five clear steps. Each one ends with a deliverable in your inbox.

  1. 01 Day 1

    Scoping Call

    15-minute call with Murtaza. We confirm what you’re building, your site, and your target window.

    You receive: a scoping summary email within 24 hours.

  2. 02 Week 1

    Site Walk

    Murtaza or Jack walks the site, takes measurements, and identifies risks before pricing.

    You receive: a risk + scope memo with photos.

  3. 03 Week 2

    Itemized Proposal

    Line-by-line cost, timeline, allowances, and exclusions. No mystery line items.

    You receive: a signed proposal and contract.

  4. 04 Weeks 3+

    Build

    Daily field logs, weekly owner reports, and a single named SAIBAY point of contact.

    You receive: weekly progress reports with photos.

  5. 05 Final Week

    Closeout

    Punch list, commissioning docs, warranty package, and final walkthrough.

    You receive: closeout binder + warranty + 1-year check-in.
